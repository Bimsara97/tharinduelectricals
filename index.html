About Us

Your Local Electrical Partner in Matara

New Tharindu Electricals is a registered individual business in Sri Lanka, located at No 144, Mahaovita, Palatuwa, Matara. We proudly serve customers in and around Thihagoda and the greater Matara district.

Our business focuses on supplying a wide range of electrical equipment, wiring items, lighting, switches, and complete sound system solutions – all at competitive prices with friendly, personalized service.

Established in 2025, we are committed to becoming the most trusted electrical supplier in the region, offering quality products and expert guidance for every project.
